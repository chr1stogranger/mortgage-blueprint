Summary: Mortgage Blueprint does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. All loan calculations and scenarios are processed locally on your device or in your browser session.

1. Introduction

Mortgage Blueprint ("the App") is a mortgage calculator and educational tool developed by Chris Granger, a licensed mortgage broker (NMLS #952015) operating under Xpert Home Lending, Inc. (NMLS #2179191). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information across our web application (blueprint.realstack.app) and mobile applications (iOS and Android).

We take your privacy seriously. The App is designed as a calculator and educational tool — not a data collection platform.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit the following:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or other personal identifiers
  • Social Security numbers, dates of birth, or government-issued IDs
  • Bank account numbers, credit card numbers, or financial account details
  • Credit reports or credit scores (any FICO score you enter stays on your device)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Contacts, photos, or files from your device

3. Information Processed Locally

When you use the App, the following data is entered by you and processed entirely on your device or within your browser session:

  • Loan scenario inputs (purchase price, down payment, interest rate, loan term, etc.)
  • Property details (address, zip code, property type)
  • Borrower profile details (FICO score, income, debts)
  • PricePoint game scores and progress

This data is stored in your browser's local storage or device memory. It is never transmitted to our servers and is deleted when you clear your browser data or uninstall the App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App connects to the following external services to provide live data. These connections transmit only the minimum information needed to fulfill the request:

FRED API (Federal Reserve Economic Data)

Used to display current mortgage interest rates. The App sends a rate data request to the FRED API. No personal information is included in this request. FRED's privacy policy is available at fred.stlouisfed.org/legal.

RapidAPI (Real Estate Data)

Used by the PricePoint feature to display real estate listings for the price-guessing game. The App sends zip code queries to retrieve public listing data (property photos, descriptions, and listing prices). No personal information is included. RapidAPI's privacy policy is available at rapidapi.com/privacy.

Vercel (Hosting)

The App is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and access timestamps as part of normal web hosting operations. Vercel's privacy policy is available at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use cookies, analytics trackers, advertising pixels, or any third-party tracking scripts. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar services. We do not track your behavior across websites or apps.

6. Data Sharing and Sales

We do not sell, rent, lease, or share your personal information with any third party. Period.

The App does not participate in data broker networks, lead generation services, or advertising networks. Your loan scenarios and financial inputs are never transmitted to lenders, real estate agents, or any other parties.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is a financial calculator intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us and we will address the concern promptly.

8. Security

Because the App processes data locally on your device, your financial information never travels over the internet to our servers. The App connects to third-party APIs over HTTPS (encrypted connections) for rate data and property listings only.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is typically no data for us to provide, modify, or delete. However, if you have any questions about your data or wish to exercise any privacy rights under applicable law (including the California Consumer Privacy Act), please contact us using the information below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. If we make material changes to how we handle personal information, we will provide notice within the App.
